Welcome to Backyard Birds

Welcome to Utah Backyard Birds, where the beauty of nature comes alive through stunning photography and artistry. Founded by renowned photographer and artist Bart Jones, our mission is to share the magnificent diversity of Utah's bird species with nature lovers everywhere.

What We Offer

Our collection features breathtaking photographs and watercolor artwork of local Utah birds, carefully crafted into beautiful calendars and postcard books. Each piece captures the unique character and vibrant colors of these amazing creatures in their natural habitat.

Featured Birds

Throughout our galleries, you'll discover a wide variety of bird species including:

  • Rufous Hummingbirds - Tiny jewels of the sky
  • Great Blue Herons - Majestic wading birds
  • American Bald Eagles - Symbols of strength and freedom
  • Cardinals - Bright red beauties
  • Robins - Cheerful harbingers of spring
  • Great Horned Owls - Silent hunters of the night

Our Products

Browse our selection of nature-inspired products:

  • Postcard Books - Perfect for sharing the beauty of Utah's birds with friends and family
  • Framed Prints - Museum-quality prints of Bart's finest work
  • Greeting Cards - Unique cards for every occasion
